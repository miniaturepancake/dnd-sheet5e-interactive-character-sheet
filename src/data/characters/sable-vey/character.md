# Sable — Shadar-kai College of Spirits Bard 11

## Part 1. Character concept, background, and story

### Core identity

- **Name:** Sable Vey
- **Species:** Shadar-kai
- **Class:** Bard 11
- **Subclass:** College of Spirits *(Unearthed Arcana 2025 version, using the 2024 PHB bard chassis)*
- **Background:** **Harper Field Archivist** *(custom 2024-style background)*
- **Origin feat:** **Magic Initiate (Wizard)**
- **Role in party:** face, investigator, occult support, control caster, spirit medium
- **Faction:** Harpers

### Character summary

Sable is a Shadar-kai Harper field archivist: a spirit medium, historian, and collector of forbidden knowledge. He is sent into ruined estates, cursed villages, battlefields, and sealed libraries to recover testimonies, last words, vanished records, and dangerous histories that safer scholars refuse to touch. Calm, precise, and faintly unsettling, he treats the dead as witnesses and the past as evidence.

### Personality

- Speaks quietly and exactly; rarely wastes a word.
- Polite to ghosts, patient with frightened people, ruthless with liars.
- Keeps ledgers, scraps, names, dates, and final statements.
- Sees archives as moral objects: if a truth is erased, someone profits.

### Backstory blurb

Sable’s work for the Harpers sits at the edge of scholarship and fieldwork. He is the one sent after the fire, after the massacre, after the haunting, after the cover-up. He interviews survivors, records the dead, and secures texts too dangerous for ordinary shelves. Candlekeep respects him because he brings back things no librarian can safely go fetch. Wisteria Vale fits his assignment profile too well: a vanished town, a sealed demiplane, a corrupted bard, and ten years of silenced testimony.

### Suggested table-facing vibe

Think **occult case officer**, not tavern minstrel and not academic librarian. Sable is the Harper who arrives with a wax-sealed folio, a travel ledger, a packet of black candles, and a deck of tarot cards used for both divination and interrogation.

### Class features

#### Bardic Inspiration (d10)

- As a **Bonus Action**, choose one creature within 60 feet who can see or hear you.
- That creature gains one Bardic Inspiration die.
- Within the next **hour**, when it fails a **D20 Test**, it can roll the die and add it to the d20, potentially turning failure into success.
- Uses: equal to your **Charisma modifier** (**5**); recover all on a Long Rest, and on a Short Rest after level 5.

#### Spellcasting

- You prepare **16 Bard spells of level 1+**.
- **Spirit Guardians** is always prepared from your subclass and does **not** count against that prepared total.
- You have Ritual Casting for Bard spells with the Ritual tag.
- Your spellcasting ability is **Charisma**.

#### Expertise

- Double proficiency bonus with **Persuasion, Investigation, Insight,** and **Perception**.

#### Jack of All Trades

- Add half your Proficiency Bonus to any ability check using a skill proficiency you lack and that does not already use your Proficiency Bonus.

#### Font of Inspiration

- Recover Bardic Inspiration on a **Short or Long Rest**.
- You may also expend a spell slot **with no action** to regain one expended use of Bardic Inspiration.

#### Countercharm

- If you or a creature within 30 feet fails a saving throw against an effect applying **Charmed** or **Frightened**, you can use your **Reaction** to cause the save to be rerolled with **Advantage**.

#### Magical Secrets

- Whenever your Bard prepared-spell total increases, your new prepared spells may come from the **Bard, Cleric, Druid,** or **Wizard** spell lists.
- When you replace a prepared Bard spell, the replacement may also come from those lists.

### Quick tactical notes

- Spell blocks below reflect Sable’s **current level-11 loadout**, not every intermediate spell choice from the progression section.
- Open social scenes with **Guidance**, **Insight**, **Investigation**, and **Persuasion**.
- Use **Circle of Power**, **See Invisibility**, and your item-granted utility spells to solve dangerous magical scenes without forcing combat.
- In combat, use **Spirits from Beyond** as your Bonus Action and keep your Action free for control spells.
- **Mind Sliver** is an excellent setup cantrip before a save-based spell on the following turn or before an ally’s save-based effect.
- **Shield** gives you a real defensive reaction that ordinary bards often lack.
- **Spirit Guardians** is your emergency concentration anchor when melee pressure appears.
- **Blessing of the Raven Queen** lets you reposition, survive focus fire, and keep concentration.
- **Counterspell**, **Dispel Magic**, **See Invisibility**, and **Faerie Fire** are your anti-illusion / anti-trick package.
- **Fochlucan Bandore** adds free casts of **Fly, Invisibility, Levitate, Protection from Evil and Good, Entangle, Faerie Fire, Shillelagh,** and **Speak with Animals**.
- **Polymorph** is your high-flexibility emergency spell: rescue, lockdown, or brute-force repositioning.
- **Raulothim's Psychic Lance** is your single-target answer to dangerous casters or isolated bosses.
- **Otto's Irresistible Dance** is your hard single-target control option when one enemy absolutely needs to be shut down.
- **Dirge** is your late-slot attrition/control spell when the fight collapses into a wide, ugly scrum around you.

### Short session-0 summary

Sable Vey is a Shadar-kai Harper field archivist and spirit medium. He is sent into haunted or forbidden places to recover dangerous texts, record testimony, and preserve truths that others would rather bury. Calm, exacting, and difficult to rattle, he treats the dead as witnesses and the past as evidence.
